ABOUT THE FIRM
The Law Firm of Steven H. Kuhn's practice is focused on litigation and transactional work in the areas of intellectual property, medical, financial, employment, and bankruptcy law. We represent a broad spectrum of clients, including patent owners, licensees, manufacturers, medical practitioners, and other individuals and entities.

Our clients come to us because of our experience, knowledge, expertise, and track record in and out of court as well as our reputation for integrity and client satisfaction. We recognize that a close working relationship between attorney and client is essential to provide quality legal services.

The firm's size and composition enables us to offer the personalized services of experienced lawyers to serve each client's unique needs.

Our rates are competitive and we are able to deliver cost-effective legal services. When a client is involved in a dispute, our primary concern is to achieve an expeditious and economic and economic result.

When formal litigation or other dispute-resolution proceedings are necessary, we provide aggressive, high caliber advocacy.

Background

David Kuhn has been practicing law since 1971. After law school, Mr. Kuhn worked as an associate at Carter, Ledyard & Milburn on Wall Street in New York. He then moved west to San Francisco where he worked as counsel for Itel Corporation where he honed his skills as a corporate attorney as well as a litigator and transactional business attorney. In 1986, he left Itel to start his own law practice, and in 2009 became Of Counsel to the Law Firm of Steven H. Kuhn.
